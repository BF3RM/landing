How to play

Reality Mod uses Venice Unleashed - a modding and improvement framework for Battlefield 3. We've been working closely together with the VU Devs since the very start. The RM team helped requesting, testing and engineering new features that were required for a complete modding toolset.


You'll need a legit, licensed and up-to-date version of Battlefield 3™ with all DLCs installed to play Reality Mod

1
Install and launch Battlefield 3™ at least once
2
Download and install the Venice Unleashed Client
3
Launch the Venice Unleashed Client, create your account and link it to your Origin Make sure you run Origin / EA App in the background
4
Join a Reality Mod server of your choice and start playing
5
Join our discord server If you are having troubles installing or playing Reality Mod feel free to contact us here

About the mod

Our fundamental goal is to create an immersive gameplay and a more realistic experience. We are designing and developing all aspects of the game around this goal.

SETTING

BF3: RM is a BF3 modification with focus on team play, communication & combined arms. It's set in the real world starting from the end of the Cold War to modern day conflicts

GAMEPLAY

The mod draws most of its inspiration from the successful BF2 "Project Reality" mod, and offers a complete gameplay and UI overhaul that creates a more tactical and in-depth experience compared to vanilla BF3

GOAL

To create an immersive gameplay and a more realistic experience. We are designing and developing all aspects of the game around this goal.
